## What is MongoDB Atlas?

Think of MongoDB Atlas as a **free online storage** for your chess app. It's like a cloud folder where your app saves:
- User accounts (names, emails, passwords)
- Chess games (who's playing, game moves, etc.)

**It's completely FREE** and you don't need to install anything on your computer!

---

## Step 1: Create Your Free Account (5 minutes)

### What You'll See:
When you click the link, you'll see a sign-up page that looks like a regular website registration.

### Detailed Steps:

1. **Open your web browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, etc.)

2. **Click this link** (or copy and paste into your browser):
   ```
   https://www.mongodb.com/cloud/atlas/register
   ```

3. **You'll see a sign-up form**. Fill it out:
   - **First Name**: Your first name
   - **Last Name**: Your last name
   - **Email**: Your email address (use a real one - you'll need to verify it)
   - **Password**: Create a password (write it down somewhere safe)
   - **Company/Project Name**: You can put "Chess App" or anything
   - **Check the box** to agree to terms (usually at the bottom)

4. **Click "Get started free"** or "Sign up" button (usually green or blue)

5. **Check your email inbox** for a verification email from MongoDB
   - Look in your spam/junk folder if you don't see it
   - Click the verification link in the email
   - This confirms your email is real

6. **After clicking the verification link**, you'll be taken back to MongoDB's website
   - You might need to log in with the email and password you just created

### Common Issues:

**"I don't see the sign-up page"**
- Make sure you clicked the link: https://www.mongodb.com/cloud/atlas/register
- Try copying and pasting it into your browser's address bar

**"I didn't get the verification email"**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Wait 2-3 minutes (sometimes emails are delayed)
- Make sure you typed your email correctly

**"I forgot my password"**
- Click "Forgot password" on the login page
- They'll send you a reset link

**"The page won't load"**
- Check your internet connection
- Try a different browser
- Try again in a few minutes
