# üìù Step-by-Step: Creating MongoDB Atlas Account

## Visual Guide (What You'll See)

### Step 1.1: Open the Website
1. Open your web browser
2. In the address bar (top of browser), type or paste:
   ```
   https://www.mongodb.com/cloud/atlas/register
   ```
3. Press Enter

### Step 1.2: The Sign-Up Page
You'll see a page that says something like "Start building with MongoDB Atlas" or "Create your account"

**What to look for:**
- A form with boxes to fill in
- Usually has fields for: First Name, Last Name, Email, Password
- A button that says "Get started free" or "Sign up"

**What to do:**
1. Fill in your **First Name** (e.g., "John")
2. Fill in your **Last Name** (e.g., "Smith")
3. Fill in your **Email** (e.g., "john@email.com")
   - Use a real email you can access!
4. Create a **Password**
   - Make it strong (mix of letters, numbers, symbols)
   - Write it down somewhere safe!
5. You might see a "Company" or "Project Name" field
   - You can type "Chess App" or leave it blank
6. Check any boxes that say "I agree to terms" or similar
7. Click the **"Get started free"** or **"Sign up"** button

### Step 1.3: Email Verification
After clicking sign up:
1. **Check your email** (the one you used to sign up)
2. Look for an email from "MongoDB" or "MongoDB Atlas"
3. **Open the email**
4. **Click the link** inside the email (usually says "Verify email" or "Confirm account")
5. This will open a new page that says "Email verified" or similar

### Step 1.4: Log In
After verifying your email:
1. You might be automatically logged in, OR
2. You'll need to log in manually:
   - Go to: https://cloud.mongodb.com
   - Enter your email
   - Enter your password
   - Click "Sign In"

### Step 1.5: You're In!
If you see a page with:
- A sidebar on the left
- Options like "Database", "Network Access", "Database Access"
- Maybe a message about creating your first cluster

**Congratulations! You've completed Step 1!** üéâ
